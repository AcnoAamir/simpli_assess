We created an e-commerce portal that lets people shop basic items on their website. The website has the following features:

User Side - 

1. Login into user profile
2. Select items from the list of items provided that you would like to buy
3. Enter the quantity of that item you wanna purchase
4. Go to cart when you have added all the items you wanna buy
5. It shows you the items and quantity of all items
6. When you press Checkout, it shows you the amount of money you need to pay and total amount to be payed
7. On pressing Payment it pays the amount


Admin side - 

1. Admin login page 
2. Admin can change password after login if they wants to
2. A master list of food items available for purchase
3. A functionality to add or remove food items

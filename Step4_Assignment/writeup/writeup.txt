Build a React app where users can view and promote upcoming events.

Background of the problem statement: 

You are a web developer at a company called FindMyMovies. The company has decided to launch a new app called FindMyEvents where users can view and add upcoming events in their region. bBackend development has been outsourced and frontend will be developed in-house. During sprint planning, you agree to lead this project and develop an app that will let users find events using APIs that backend engineers will provide.

Features -
Has a login page
Has recent events listed
Has location of office
Has review of event and details of event
